About Us

Dr. Harley Mutisya founded REGIONAL SUPPLY CHAIN TRAINERS LIMITED as part of the Joyley Group of Companies.

The company was Incorporated in Nairobi to carry out consultancy services to manufacturing industries, merchandising firms, government and non-governmental organizations on matters related to procurement and supply chain management.

Company Statistics:


Employees: 24
Year Founded: 2024
Ownership: Private

Company Strengths

Assets: The Company has trucks, pick-ups, and hires on need basis to cater for the distribution needs of our customers.
Customers: The company has very strong affiliation and relationships with tanks manufacturers, construction and building material Firms: enabling effective transport and distribution of goods to most parts of the country except in certain risk prone zones or non-motorable roads.
Ownership: Company is owned by Local Directors whose roots are Kenyan; with proper understanding of needs of all their customers.
